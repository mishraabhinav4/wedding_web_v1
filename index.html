Organization

01

Baraat and Dwar Puja

The celebration starts with the Baraat. Upon arrival, the Dwar Puja is performed, welcoming him with traditional rituals and blessings for the couple as they begin their new life together. Join us in this joyful celebration of love and togetherness!

02

Jaimaala

Join us for our enchanting Jaimala ceremony, where we will exchange floral garlands, symbolizing our eternal love and devotion. Your presence will bless this sacred moment as we begin our divine journey of love and companionship.

03

Mangal Phere & Sindoor Daan

Be a part of our sacred Mangal Phere and Sindoor Daan ceremonies, where we take the Agni ke Mangal Phere, making vows to love and support each other. As the groom applies sindoor to the bride, it symbolizes our union and the start of our life together as husband and wife.

04

Vidaayi and Blessings - A New Beginning

Be with us for the heartfelt Vidai ceremony, a moment of both joy and tender farewells, as the bride departs with her husband to start a new chapter of life. This emotional tradition symbolizes the blessings of family and loved ones, sending the couple forth with love, prayers, and cherished memories. As we step into this journey together, your warm wishes and presence add profound meaning to this new beginning.
